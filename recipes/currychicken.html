Curry Chicken

This delicious main entree recipe willgo perfect with the other recipes featured on this site.

Curry Chicken is an island staple. Bite sized pieces of chicken simmered in a delicious curry sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2½ lbs of chicken thighs
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 2 tbsp All-purpose or Chicken seasoning
  • 1 ½ Salt
  • 5 Allspice berries
  • 3 cloves of garlic crushed
  • 1 medium onionchopped
  • 3 sprigs of fresh thyme
  • 2 medium carrotschopped
  • 1 small potatodiced
  • 1 ½ tsp freshly grated ginger
  • 1 small scotch bonnet pepper
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil or vegetable oil
  • boiling water

Instructions

  1. In a kitchen bowl, add the chicken, onion, garlic, scotch bonnet pepper, pimento berries, ginger, salt, all-purpose seasoning, the curry powder and mix everything together and leave to marinate for 30 minutes or more.
  2. In a pot add the cooking oil and put to hot on medium heat
  3. Add the seasoned chicken to the oil and stir well. Let the chicken cook until lightly brown on all sides. Don't let the pot burn, add a tip of water if necessary.
  4. Stir in the carrot, potato and thyme. Add water enough to cover the chicken in the pot. Cover and leave to cook for 25-30 minutes, until the chicken is cooked and the water is reduced to a curry gravy.

serve with rice and peas
