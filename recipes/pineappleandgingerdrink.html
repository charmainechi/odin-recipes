Pineapple and Ginger Drink

A delicious tropical drink packed with health benefits and can be drunk with any meal or at any time!

Pineapple and Ginger are two staple island ingredients and this drink does a great job of using these two ingredients to create something truly magical and tasty!

Ingredients

  • 10 cups ripe pineapple chopped
  • 5 - 6 cups water
  • 3 - 4 inch ginger root chopped
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup sugar or honey to taste

Instructions

This recipe makes up to 7 cups
  1. Add half (approximately 5 cups) of the chopped pineapple to the blender.
  2. Add the ginger root to the blender.
  3. Add half of the water to the blender.
  4. Blend on high for approximately 15 seconds or until the pineapple pieces are smooth
  5. Strain the juice into a large bowl and discard the dry pulp left over in the strainer.
  6. Repeat the process with the remaining half of the pineapple and the remaining water.
  7. Add the lemon juice and sugar (or honey) and stir the mixture.
  8. Taste the mixture to see if it needs more sweetener or water and a little of the desired ingredient until you have get to your desired taste preference.
  9. Pour the mixture into a sealed container and refrigerate for up to 3 days.
  10. Serve cold and enjoy!
