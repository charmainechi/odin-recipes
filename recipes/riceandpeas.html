Rice and Peas

A delicious rice recipe that is a staple with any Caribbean main entree

Rice and peas is a recipe of rice and beans simmered in coconut milk and spices to create the ideal starch to accompany any Caribbean meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp canola or vegetable oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp allspice powder
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper
  • 2 x 14oz cans red kidney beans drained
  • 1 x 14oz coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cups long grain white rice

Instructions

  1. Add oil to a pot over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic, onion, thyme, all spice, bay leaves, scotch bonnet to the pot and cook until onion is translucent
  3. Add Add coconut milk, water, beans and salt. Once it comes to a simmer, add rice then give it a quick stir.
  4. Turn heat to low then simmer until all the liquid is absorbed.
  5. Rest for 15 minutes, then fluff and serve!

Serve with curry chicken
